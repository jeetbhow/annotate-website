Features

Effortless Browsing Meets Powerful Annotation

Our browser extension is engineered to blend seamlessly with your everyday browsing. Without altering your browsing speed or behaviour, it integrates directly into your browser, ensuring that powerful annotation tools are always at your fingertips, whenever you need them.

Enhance Teamwork with Shared Annotations

Whether you’re working on a group project, conducting joint research, or just sharing interesting finds, our extension’s collaborative features make it simple and efficient. Share your annotations with anyone to foster better communication and teamwork.

Your Annotations, Your Way

Customize how you highlight and annotate web pages to suit your style and needs. With a variety of tools and styles at your disposal, our extension makes it easy to emphasize and organize the information that matters most to you.
